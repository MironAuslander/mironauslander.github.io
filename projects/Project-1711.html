Project Overview

Fauda is a suspense drama starring Lior Raz that follows the life of a commander in an elite undercover counter-terrorism units. The series is known for its authenticity, which was expressed in the creation of the effects and the design of elements that faithfully accompany the work and intense lives of the members of this special unit. The project's success was almost immediate, highlighting the compelling nature of its storytelling and production.
